About ME

Amoabeng Augustus Ampaw is my name.
A level 200 student of Koforidua Technical University studying procurement and supply Chain Management. Augustus is a person who assist students in their academics and also offer advice to students which goes a long way to shape their academic journey. Augustus takes students through tutorials on courses such as Accounting, macro economics , Quantitative Methods and other related procurement courses with a rate free. I am currently an aspirant for PASSG president in the upcoming election 25/26.

As a passionate student of procurement and supply, I am committed to fostering an environment of excellence and innovation within our association. My vision for PASSAG is rooted in the principles of Total Quality Management (TQM). I believe that by embracing a culture of continuous improvement, collaboration and accountability, we can elevate our standards and achieve remarkable outcomes. Together, we can enhance our academic experiences, strengthen our professional networks and prepare ourselves to be leaders in the field of procurement and supply. I am dedicated to creating opportunities for skill development, knowledge sharing and meaningful engagement among our members. With your support, I will work tirelessly to ensure that our voices are heard and our aspirations are realized. Let us embark on this journey together towards a brighter future for PASSAG. I humbly ask for your vote and support in this upcoming election. Thank you.
